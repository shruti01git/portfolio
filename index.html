I'm a final-year engineering student who fell deep into the world of visual design — and never looked back. My background in electronics gave me a systems-level way of thinking that I now apply to how I design products and interfaces.

Over an 8-month AI internship at Parabola9, I went from rough wireframes to a production component system, learning that great design is about decisions: what to leave out, how to guide attention, and why white space is never wasted.

I'm fluent in Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva — and comfortable enough with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to speak the same language as engineers when handing off designs.
